About

QKNJAK SHKODER.

Our organization has begun its work with the target group of disabled people since 1989. Our organization is registered in all institutions like Tribunal and Tax Authority. It has obtained the licence from National Social Service of Ministry of Work, Social Issues and Equal Chances, concerning services offered to disabled people. There is also in process to obtain license for the offered professional courses and for High School to Ministry of Education. The scope of these activities was the life betterment of the disabled people and education of other persons that could contribute for them. In recent years, through our commitment, finally the engagement of the disabled protagonists has been spread in local and national activities. The walls of total separation were finally broken to begin the challenge of the disabled inclusion as equal in the middle of equals. We highlight the life of the disabled people in the country and so we engage(d) in activities where were included all the ages and invited local disabled people. Media was a good ally in all our activities seeing us as a special organization to be supported and not being forgotten. Our sectors of acting are: Health (In general), secondary and post-secondary education, health personnel development, strengthening civil society, awakening of human rights consciousness, women development and promotion of development awareness. Our target groups are: children less than 18 years old, community based organizations, disabled people and professionals working with these groups, students, women and young people. Our activity has been implemented successfully in the Shkodra Prefecture. We can mention to have a cross-reference of our experience regarding disabled people rehabilitation, their education, human rights education in the Shkodra, Malesia e Madhe and Puke Disctricts.  As we are a non-profit organization, our funding necessity has been accomplished by EU member states public bodies and International organizations.  A special help to our organization has been the volunteers work. Our organization has engaged professionals from the University Luigj Gurakuqi, Shkoder. This university provides us the necessary volunteers, students from this university, which can do their internship in our organization, during our frequent project.

We work passionately since 1989.
 Head of QKNJAK
Antonjeta PERO
